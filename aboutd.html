About Us

The Disaster Prediction and Relief Coordination Database is conceived as a highly scalable and intelligent relational database management system (RDBMS) aimed at transforming disaster management. By integrating comprehensive data storage, advanced predictive analytics, and real-time coordination mechanisms, the system establishes a unified and resilient digital framework for managing crises.

Our Vision & Mission

Our mission is to enhance disaster preparedness, optimize relief operations, and empower communities through advanced data analytics, geospatial visualization, and real-time coordination.

Key Features

Predictive Analytics

Leverages machine learning models to forecast potential disasters early, enabling proactive planning and timely response.

Geospatial Visualization

Maps high-risk areas, shelters, and resource centers using interactive mapping APIs for quick and informed decision-making.

Real-Time Coordination

Automated triggers and alerts enable administrators and volunteers to coordinate relief efforts efficiently during emergencies.

Role-Based Access

Admins manage resources and operations while users access critical information regarding shelters, resources, and safety measures.

Optimized Resource Allocation

Ensures live inventory updates and demand forecasting for effective distribution of relief materials and services.
